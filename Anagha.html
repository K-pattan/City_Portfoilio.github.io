Recreation & Lifestyle

Service Overview

Recreation services include parks, walking tracks, open gyms, cultural spaces and community halls that enhance physical health, mental well-being and social interaction in urban environments.

Why this service is important

  • Reduces stress caused by dense urban living
  • Encourages physical fitness across age groups
  • Creates inclusive public spaces

Data Structures Used

  • Graph – city zones and parks
  • Queue – level-wise traversal
  • Array – distance storage

Ensures equal accessibility of recreational spaces from all zones.
