Smart City Services – Digital Infrastructure & Public Welfare

1. Recreation & Lifestyle Services

In Maharajpur, recreational facilities are planned to ensure equal access across all residential zones. Parks, walking tracks, and community spaces are distributed such that no household is isolated from lifestyle amenities.

  • Community parks and open gyms
  • Walking and cycling paths
  • Local activity and cultural centers
Algorithm Used: Breadth First Search (BFS)
Why: Ensures uniform accessibility across all zones
Time Complexity: O(V + E)

2. Public Safety & Emergency Services

Public safety in Maharajpur is designed using layered monitoring. Surveillance systems and emergency services are placed strategically to reduce response time and cover critical regions.

  • Smart CCTV surveillance
  • Emergency response units
  • Well-lit streets and public areas
Algorithm Used: Dijkstra’s Algorithm
Why: Finds the shortest response path during emergencies
Time Complexity: O(E log V)

3. Digital Citizen Services

Maharajpur’s digital services aim to minimize manual effort and provide fast access to essential services through a unified digital platform.

  • Single digital portal for citizens
  • Online service request management
  • Digital identity and records
Algorithm Used: Hashing
Why: Enables constant-time service lookup
Time Complexity: O(1)

4. Senior Citizen & Accessibility Support

Special emphasis is placed on senior citizens and differently-abled individuals. Services are prioritized to ensure comfort, safety, and faster assistance.

  • Priority medical access
  • Accessible public infrastructure
  • Support kiosks
Algorithm Used: Priority Queue (Heap)
Why: Ensures urgent needs are addressed first
Time Complexity: O(log n)

5. Smart Water Management

Water management in Maharajpur focuses on sustainability and reliability. Monitoring and distribution systems ensure consistent supply and early detection of inefficiencies.

  • Leak detection mechanisms
  • Consumption monitoring
  • Efficient distribution planning
Algorithm Used: Greedy Strategy
Why: Allocates water resources efficiently
Time Complexity: O(n)
