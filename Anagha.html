Smart City Services – Maharajpur

Recreation & Lifestyle

Maharajpur includes parks, walking tracks, and community spaces to improve mental and physical well-being. These facilities are distributed evenly across the city to ensure accessibility for all residents.

Algorithm: Breadth First Search (BFS)
Usage: Ensures equal reachability of recreation spaces from all zones
Time Complexity: O(V + E)

Public Safety & Services

Surveillance systems, emergency units, and service centers are positioned to minimize response time during emergencies and ensure city-wide safety.

Algorithm: Dijkstra’s Algorithm
Usage: Computes shortest emergency response paths
Time Complexity: O(E log V)

Digital Services

A centralized digital platform allows residents to access services such as applications, complaints, and records efficiently.

Algorithm: Hashing
Usage: Fast retrieval of citizen records and services
Time Complexity: O(1)

Senior Citizen & Disabled-Friendly Services

Priority-based service systems ensure elderly and differently-abled individuals receive timely assistance.

Algorithm: Priority Queue (Heap)
Usage: Handles service requests based on urgency
Time Complexity: O(log n)

Smart Water Management

Efficient monitoring and distribution strategies ensure sustainable water supply throughout Maharajpur.

Algorithm: Greedy Technique
Usage: Optimal allocation of water resources
Time Complexity: O(n)
