Smart City Services – Maharajpur

Recreation & Lifestyle

Parks, walking tracks, and community spaces improve physical and mental well-being. Facilities are distributed evenly across city zones to ensure accessibility.

Purpose: Promote health, eco-friendly habits, and social interaction.
Implementation: Smart mapping ensures equal access, IoT-enabled lighting improves safety, and fitness stations encourage activity.
Extra Point: Green zones also improve air quality and biodiversity, making the city healthier overall.

Algorithm: Breadth First Search (BFS)
Usage: Ensures equal reachability from residential zones.
Time Complexity: O(V + E)

Public Safety & Services

AI cameras, drone monitoring, and emergency units ensure rapid response to incidents anywhere in the city.

Purpose: Minimize response time and monitor traffic/events.
Implementation: AI detection, drone surveillance, panic buttons, and optimized routing for emergency vehicles.
Extra Point: Predictive analytics forecast crowd density during festivals or traffic jams to prevent accidents.

Algorithm: Dijkstra’s Algorithm
Usage: Computes shortest paths for emergency and drone routing.
Time Complexity: O(E log V)

Digital Services

Centralized platforms like citizen portals, traffic apps, smart parking, and metro scheduling make services efficient and transparent.

Purpose: Deliver services quickly and transparently.
Implementation: Digital dashboards with AI chatbots, multilingual support, and secure blockchain records.
Extra Point: Voice-enabled services ensure accessibility for seniors and differently-abled citizens.

Algorithm: Hashing
Usage: Fast retrieval of citizen records, traffic updates, and metro schedules.
Time Complexity: O(1)

Senior Citizen & Disabled-Friendly Services

Priority systems ensure elderly and differently-abled individuals receive timely assistance with accessible transport, medical services, and dedicated queues.

Purpose: Inclusivity and prompt attention.
Implementation: Wheelchair ramps, tactile paths, wearable health alerts, and priority transport booking.
Extra Point: Emergency alerts linked to medical wearables notify hospitals instantly during health crises.

Algorithm: Priority Queue (Heap)
Usage: Handles service requests by urgency.
Time Complexity: O(log n)

Smart Water Management

Monitors water supply, detects leaks, and distributes water efficiently using sensors and smart valves.

Purpose: Sustainable water usage, reduce wastage, ensure supply.
Implementation: Leak detection sensors, predictive analytics, rainwater harvesting, and mobile usage tracking.
Extra Point: Citizens can monitor consumption via apps, encouraging conservation and lowering bills.

Algorithm: Greedy Technique
Usage: Allocates water based on demand and priority.
Time Complexity: O(n)
