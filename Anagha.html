Smart City Services – Maharajpur

Recreation & Lifestyle

Parks, walking tracks, and community spaces improve physical and mental well-being. Smart mapping ensures every zone has access. IoT lighting and fitness stations enhance safety and engagement.

Purpose: Promote health, eco-friendly habits, and social interaction.
Implementation: BFS algorithm ensures equal reachability from residential zones.

Algorithm: Breadth First Search (BFS)
Usage: Ensures equal reachability from residential zones.
Time Complexity: O(V + E)

Public Safety & Services

AI cameras, drones, and emergency units ensure rapid response. Panic buttons and predictive analytics prevent incidents.

Purpose: Minimize response time and monitor traffic/events.
Implementation: Dijkstra’s algorithm routes emergency vehicles efficiently.

Algorithm: Dijkstra’s Algorithm
Usage: Computes shortest paths for emergency and drone routing.
Time Complexity: O(E log V)

Digital Services

Citizen portals, traffic apps, smart parking, and metro scheduling make services accessible and transparent. AI chatbots and blockchain ensure speed and security.

Purpose: Efficient and transparent service delivery.
Implementation: Hashing enables fast access to records and updates.

Algorithm: Hashing
Usage: Fast retrieval of citizen records, traffic updates, and metro schedules.
Time Complexity: O(1)

Senior Citizen & Disabled-Friendly Services

Accessible transport, medical support, and priority queues ensure dignity and care. Wearables and ramps improve mobility and safety.

Purpose: Inclusivity and prompt attention.
Implementation: Priority queues handle requests based on urgency.

Algorithm: Priority Queue (Heap)
Usage: Handles service requests by urgency.
Time Complexity: O(log n)

Smart Water Management

Sensors detect leaks, smart valves regulate flow, and apps track usage. Rainwater harvesting and predictive analytics ensure sustainability.

Purpose: Sustainable water usage, reduce wastage, ensure supply.
Implementation: Greedy allocation based on demand and priority.

Algorithm: Greedy Technique
Usage: Allocates water based on demand and priority.
Time Complexity: O(n)
