Recreation & Lifestyle

The city features community parks, open-air gyms, walking trails, weekend markets, and activity centers. These spaces are designed to improve physical wellbeing and support social interaction.

Public Safety & Services

AI-powered CCTV cameras, drone monitoring, emergency response systems, and smart streetlights ensure a safer living environment. SOS stations are placed across the city for immediate help.

Digital Services

A unified citizen portal, smart parking system, digital ID services, and traffic monitoring apps make the city more connected and user-friendly. Residents can access all services from a single platform.

Senior Citizen & Accessibility Services

The city offers ramps, voice-assisted kiosks, low-floor transport, and priority medical support to ensure accessibility and comfort for senior citizens and individuals with disabilities.

Smart Water Management

Automated leak detection, water quality monitoring, rainwater harvesting, and greywater recycling help maintain a sustainable and efficient water supply throughout the city.
