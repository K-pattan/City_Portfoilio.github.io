Anagha

Smart City Digital Infrastructure & Public Safety Design for Maharajpur using Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA)

Recreation & Lifestyle

Parks, fitness zones, walking trails and community hubs are distributed across Maharajpur.

Algorithm Used: Graph Traversal (BFS)
Why: Ensures equal accessibility from all residential zones.

Public Safety & Surveillance

AI CCTV, emergency SOS points and drone patrol coverage.

Algorithms: DFS, Greedy
Why: Deep exploration of sensitive zones & optimal camera placement.

Digital Citizen Services

Unified portal for IDs, parking, complaints and traffic alerts.

Algorithms: Hashing, Queue
Why: Fast lookup (O(1)) and request handling.

Senior & Accessibility Support

Priority healthcare, voice kiosks, assisted navigation.

Algorithms: Priority Queue (Heap)
Why: Critical users served first.

Smart Water Management

Leak monitoring, quality sensors, sustainability analytics.

Algorithms: Dijkstra, Graphs
Why: Efficient flow & shortest delivery paths.

Algorithms & Time Complexity Used

Algorithm Used In Time Complexity
BFS Recreation Accessibility O(V + E)
DFS Security Zone Exploration O(V + E)
Greedy Camera & Resource Placement O(E log V)
Hashing Citizen Services O(1) Average
Priority Queue (Heap) Senior & Emergency Services O(log n)
Dijkstra Utility Optimization O(E log V)
