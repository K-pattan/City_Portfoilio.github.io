MAHARAJPUR SMART CITY

Anagha

Smart City Service Design for Maharajpur using Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA). Each service is decomposed into smaller functional modules.

1. Recreation & Lifestyle

Park Accessibility Planning +

Residential zones in Maharajpur are treated as graph nodes. BFS traversal ensures equal access to parks and gyms.

Algorithm: BFS
Time Complexity: O(V + E)

Community Activity Scheduling +

Events are queued and processed efficiently.

Algorithm: Queue
Time Complexity: O(n)

2. Public Safety & Services

CCTV & Drone Surveillance +

DFS is used to deeply scan critical zones and blind spots.

Algorithm: DFS
Time Complexity: O(V + E)

Emergency Response Optimization +

Greedy technique ensures nearest emergency unit responds first.

Algorithm: Greedy
Time Complexity: O(E log V)

3. Digital Citizen Services

Fast Service Lookup +

Hash tables enable instant access to citizen services.

Algorithm: Hashing
Time Complexity: O(1)

Request Handling System +

Requests are processed in arrival order.

Algorithm: Queue
Time Complexity: O(n)

4. Senior Citizen & Accessibility Services

Priority Medical Assistance +

Priority queues ensure critical citizens are served first.

Algorithm: Heap
Time Complexity: O(log n)

5. Smart Water Management

Leak Detection & Monitoring +

Graph-based routing optimizes monitoring paths.

Algorithm: Dijkstra
Time Complexity: O(E log V)
