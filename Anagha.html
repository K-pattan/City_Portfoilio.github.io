Anagha

Smart City Services – Maharajpur

Recreation & Lifestyle

This service focuses on parks, walking tracks, and community spaces that improve physical and mental well-being. These spaces are planned so that every residential area has easy access.

Algorithm: Breadth First Search (BFS)
Why: Ensures equal reachability from all zones
Complexity: O(V + E)
Public Safety & Services

This includes AI surveillance cameras, drone monitoring, emergency response units, and control centers. AI cameras detect incidents, drones monitor large areas, and emergency vehicles are routed optimally.

Algorithm: Dijkstra’s Algorithm
Why: Finds shortest emergency response paths
Complexity: O(E log V)
Digital Services

Digital services include citizen portals, traffic apps, smart parking, metro schedules, and online complaints. All services are centralized into a single digital platform.

Algorithm: Hashing
Why: Fast access to citizen data and services
Complexity: O(1)
Senior Citizen & Disabled-Friendly Services

Priority-based systems ensure elderly and differently-abled citizens receive faster access to healthcare, transport, and government services.

Algorithm: Priority Queue (Heap)
Why: Urgent requests handled first
Complexity: O(log n)
Smart Water Management

Smart water management uses sensors to monitor supply, detect leaks, and distribute water efficiently across zones.

Algorithm: Greedy Technique
Why: Optimal allocation with minimal wastage
Complexity: O(n)
