Identified Business Ideas

Educational Institutions

Educational institutions are places where students gain knowledge and skills through organized teaching. They include schools, colleges, and universities that follow a structured curriculum. Their purpose is to support academic growth and prepare students for future careers.

Healthcare & Wellness

Health care and wellness focus on maintaining and improving physical, mental, and emotional well-being. They include medical services, healthy lifestyle practices, and preventive care. The goal is to help individuals live healthier, longer, and more balanced lives.

Eco-mobility

Eco-mobility refers to environmentally friendly ways of moving people and goods, such as walking, cycling, and using public transport or electric vehicles. It focuses on reducing pollution, saving energy, and improving urban life. The goal is to create cleaner, safer, and more sustainable transportation systems.

Residential

Residential refers to places where people live, such as homes, apartments, or hostels. These spaces provide shelter, safety, and basic living facilities. They are designed to support daily living and create a comfortable environment.

Finance

Finance refers to the management of money, investments, and financial resources. It includes activities like saving, budgeting, banking, and investing. Its main goal is to help individuals and businesses grow, plan, and use their money wisely.
