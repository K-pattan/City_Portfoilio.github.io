The City Design initiative focuses on creating a sustainable, smart, and citizen-centric urban environment. Our goal is to integrate modern technology with core municipal services to enhance livability, efficiency, and public engagement. This phased approach outlines the foundational and implementation stages of our urban transformation strategy.
