2. Algorithms & Data Structures Used

Graphs

  • City zones as vertices
  • Roads, grids, networks as edges

Dijkstra

  • Tourist routes
  • Waste collection paths
  • Energy transmission

Prim & Kruskal

  • Minimum cost infrastructure
  • Grid & network planning

Hashing

  • Citizen ID lookup
  • Tax & service records

Sorting

  • Tourist popularity
  • Industry ranking
