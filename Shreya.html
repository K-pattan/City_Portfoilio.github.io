3. Time Complexity Summary

  • Dijkstra: O(E log V)
  • Prim / Kruskal: O(E log V)
  • BFS / DFS: O(V + E)
  • Hashing: O(1) average
  • Heap Operations: O(log n)
  • Sorting: O(n log n)
